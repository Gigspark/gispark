Event Guidelines

  1. Participants must register at least 30 minutes before the event starts.
  2. All participants should carry their college ID card for verification.
  3. Mobile phones should be kept in silent mode during the event.
  4. Strictly follow the dress code mentioned in the event instructions.
  5. Participants must adhere to the time limits for each event round.
  6. Use of unfair means or misbehavior will lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. Team members should cooperate and maintain discipline during the event.
  8. All decisions made by the event coordinator are final and binding.
  9. Food and drinks are not allowed inside the event hall.
  10. Emergency contact details should be submitted to the event coordinator.
